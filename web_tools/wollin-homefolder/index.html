Here Goes Nothin'

My name is Jesse L. Wollin and I’m an artist, web designer and developer. After pursuing an education in Academic painting and drawing from the Atelier in Minneapolis I moved back to Southwest Minnesota, where I began self teaching myself web design and development. My interest in this area has grown substantially since then, however. As a result, I’ve begun to make my studies a full-time commitment at Minneapolis Community and Technical College.

It’s been great coming back to Minneapolis seeing once again how diverse living in the Twin Cities really is, especially where I live in uptown. At this time, I am in a unique position because of my past experience in fine art and because of my interest in the growing technology fields and look forward to growing toward with a challenging and rewarding career.
